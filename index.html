about

Stephanie is a web designer and front-end developer from the Bay Area in CA. In 2016, she earned her Bachelor’s degree in neuroscience and got a job at a medical office in NYC with the intention of becoming a doctor or scientist. However, her heart wasn’t in it, and she decided to instead pursue her lifelong passion for design and desire to create. When she’s not working, Steph enjoys taking spin classes, perusing fashion blogs, and traveling. She currently resides in Brooklyn, NY, with her two cats.
